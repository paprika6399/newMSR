21世纪海上丝绸之路，通常简称为海上丝绸之路(MSR)，是“一带一路”倡议的海上航线部分，同时也是中国增加投资和发展的战略举措。 21世纪海上丝绸之路的主要航线为：泉州——福州——广州——海口——北海——河内——吉隆坡——雅加达——科伦坡——加尔各答——内罗毕——雅典——威尼斯。途径国家主要为：韩国、日本、印尼、泰国、马来西亚、越南、柬埔寨、新加坡、菲律宾、缅甸、文莱、印度、斯里兰卡、巴基斯坦、科威特、沙特阿拉伯、土耳其、埃及、阿联酋、肯尼亚、坦桑尼亚、希腊、意大利等国家。海上丝绸之路倡议由中国国家主席习近平于2013年10月在印度尼西亚议会发表讲话时首次提出。

2014年11月，中国宣布设立一项发展基金，这将有助于资助中国发展新丝绸之路和海上丝绸之路的计划。中国通过在内罗毕和蒙巴萨之间快速建设现代标准轨距铁路，加快了将非洲纳入海上丝绸之路的努力。

2015年3月，中国国家发展和改革委员会公开发布了一份题为“共同建设丝绸之路经济带和21世纪海上丝绸之路的愿景与行动”的文件，讨论了构成“丝绸之路经济带”基础的原则和框架倡议。

The 21st Century Maritime Silk Road, often referred to simply as the Maritime Silk Road (MSR), is the maritime route part of the Belt and Road Initiative and a strategic move by China to increase investment and development.

The main routes of the 21st Century Maritime Silk Road are: Quanzhou - Fuzhou - Guangzhou - Haikou - Beihai - Hanoi - Kuala Lumpur - Jakarta - Colombo - Calcutta - Nairobi - Athens - Venice. The main countries are: South Korea, Japan, Indonesia, Thailand, Malaysia, Vietnam, Cambodia, Singapore, Philippines, Myanmar, Brunei, India, Sri Lanka, Pakistan, Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, Turkey, Egypt, United Arab Emirates, Kenya, Tanzania, Greece , Italy and other countries.

The Maritime Silk Road initiative was first proposed by Chinese President Xi Jinping in October 2013 in a speech to the Indonesian Parliament.

In November 2014, China announced the establishment of a development fund that will help finance China's plans to develop the New Silk Road and the Maritime Silk Road. China has accelerated efforts to include Africa in the Maritime Silk Road by rapidly building a modern standard-gauge railway between Nairobi and Mombasa.

In March 2015, the National Development and Reform Commission of China publicly released a document titled "Vision and Actions for Jointly Building the Silk Road Economic Belt and the 21st Century Maritime Silk Road", discussing the issues that constitute the "Silk Road Economic Belt".
